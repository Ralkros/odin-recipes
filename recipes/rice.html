How to make rice

Rice is a very easy and simple recipe that everyone should know. Most people do not know how to properly make rice without a rice cooker and ewven then they may not do it properly.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water

Recipe

  1. Clean rice three times
  2. Measure water to double the height of the rice in the pot. Easily done by sticking your finger down to the bottom of the pot and using your thumb as a mark.
  3. Place on high heat
  4. Once water reaches a fast boil, reduce heat to low/simmer
  5. Place a lid on top and let it cook for another 15-20 minutes
  6. Fluff rice with fork and serve once cooled
